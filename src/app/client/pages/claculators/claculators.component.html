How to specify the order details?
  1. Symbol is the trading asset you are planning to Buy or Sell. Pick from currency pairs, indices, commodities, and digital assets.
  2. the deposit currency of your trading account.
  3. Period in days is the number of days you plan to keep your order open. We do not charge swap fees for holding orders open overnight on any of our platforms, so this field is for your better understanding.
  4. Direction is whether you are planning to execute a Buy or a Sell order. Buy is when you go Long because of the rising quotes, whereas Sell order is when you go Short because of the falling quotes.
  5. Volume, lots is the volume of one order measured in standard lots. One lot is 100,000 units of the base currency. Mini lot equals 0.1 standard lots and constitutes 10,000 units of the base currency. Micro lot equals 0.01 lots and constitutes 1,000 units of the base currency.
  6. Open price is the price you plan to open your order at.
  7. Close price is the price you plan to close your order at.
How to calculate one pip?
  1. for 5-digit currency pairs—by the 4th decimal (0.0001)
  2. for 3-digit currency pairs and XAGUSD—by the 2nd decimal (0.01)
  3. for XAUUSD, XPDUSD, XBRUSD, XTIUSD—by the 1st decimal (0.1)
  4. for indices (exсept JPN225)—by the 1st decimal (0.1)
  5. for JPN225—by the 4th decimal (0.0001).
